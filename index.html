Arcade game

Collect gems with different score depenting on pickup time.
After 4 gems the "master key" spawns and you can run to the water.
Avoid the bugs under all time!
Compete with friends who gets the best time and score.

Gem scores: Orange 500, Green 300 and blue 150.

Move around with Arrow keys or W, A ,S and D.
